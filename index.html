Welcome to TiffinGo

TiffinGo is an app-based kitchen service which delivers freshly cooked tiffins packed with nutritious meals directly to schools. Students can place an order for their tiffin a night before using the TiffinGo app. The meals are prepared in our kitchens, packed, and delivered to the schools 15-30 minutes before lunchtime.

About

What Do We Do ?

TiffinGo solves the problem with a simple business model. We set up our centralised kitchen in Gurgaon. The students can get their tiffin box delivered to school by placing an order on our app a night before. Students can also pick monthly, weekly and daily plans.

  • TiffinGo eases the lives of parents & children by
  • Delivering fresh, healthy, & tasty meals directly to the school.
  • Offering a curated menu & healthy options.
  • Making nutritious meals affordable.

India is a developing country, repercussion of which is - over the last couple of decades, there has been a tectonic shift in the lifestyle of families. High-calorie diets, junk-food, processed & refined ingredients are filling our plates. India has the highest number of adult diabetes cases in the world: 77 million people (50% of the global diabetic burden). The cases are alarmingly rising among children too, primarily due to increased consumption of refined foods. School lunch is one of the most important meals of the day but is proving to be the unhealthiest for many children.
